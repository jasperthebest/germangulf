aboutGERMAN Gulf

German Gulf Engineering Consultants (GGEC) are Design and Project Management Consultants (Pre and Post Contract Services) based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, to provide sustainable, innovative and efficient solutions for the construction industry and thereby contribute to the Nation’s development.

    At GGEC extensive multidimensional expertise combines to create outstanding results time and again. Our services cover Planning and Urban development, Architecture and Engineering Design, Permit Management, Project Management and Enterprise Asset Information Management for Commercial, Residential, Retail, Infrastructure, Industrial, Entertainment, Education, Hospitality, Healthcare and Specialized construction projects.

    Together with our Associates GGEC has an enviable reputation in the field of architectural and engineering consultancy services and has the capacity to execute diverse projects such as urban development (villages and cities), residential projects varying in scale and complexity, commercial buildings, shopping malls and entertainment centres, educational institutions, hospitality developments and healthcare projects providing services that meet international and local codes and construction standards under one umbrella.

    GGEC translates global concepts into reality, compatible with local culture, traditional values and a sustainable environment. Our commitment is to deliver quality with cost effectiveness, to support progressive development and to exceed client expectations with performance excellence.

Corporate Social Responsibility

German Gulf Engineering Consultants has made the commitment to develop a Corporate Social Responsibility Plan to ensure that its daily activities adhere to a set of moral guidelines and respect people and the environment. It is the continuing commitment by the business to behave ethically, contribute to economic development and improve the quality of life of employees, their families, the local community and the wider society.

We are embarking on a program of educational modules for schools and shopping malls to enhance awareness of sustainable issues. We encourage energy and resource savings within the Company and make further contributions through investing in Carbon Offsets.

The Carbon Offsets initiative will both help in reforestation efforts and contribute to sustainable development for rural areas.
